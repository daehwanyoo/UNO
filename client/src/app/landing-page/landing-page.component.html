How to Play UNO

UNO is a card game that involves players matching colors and numbers.

  • Each player is dealt 7 cards at the start.
  • Players take turns drawing cards and matching the discard pile.
  • Special cards can change the game direction or force players to draw cards.
  • The first player to get rid of all their cards wins the round.
Action Cards

Draw 2 Cards: The next player must draw 2 cards and lose their turn.

Reverse: Reverses the direction of play.

Skip: The next player is skipped.

Wild: Allows the player to choose the color to be played next.

Wild Draw 4: The next player must draw 4 cards and lose their turn. The player also chooses the color to be played next.

You can draw a card using this card. When you have a single card left, press this card popping up next to your card to win.
